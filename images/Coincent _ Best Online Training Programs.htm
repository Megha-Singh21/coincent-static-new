Coincent 3 years program Blueprint for Accelerating Your Career

Year 1: Live Industrial Training – Build Your Foundation

Hands-on Industry Exposure from Day One

  • Live Industrial Training (2.5 Months): Real-time training in a professional environment to understand how industries operate.
  • Skill-Based Learning: Focus on the latest technologies and industry tools to prepare you for future project work.
  • Mentorship from Industry Experts: Learn directly from professionals who guide you through the practical applications of your coursework.
Year 2: Real-Time Projects – Apply What You’ve Learned

Transform Knowledge into Experience

  • 8 Real-Time Projects: Collaborate on industry-level projects to gain hands-on experience.
  • Portfolio Building: Each project adds value to your resume and professional profile.
  • Team Collaboration: Work in teams, simulating a real working environment and improving soft skills and project management.
Year 3: Internship + Placement Support – Launch Your Career

Step Into the Industry with Confidence

  • Guaranteed Internship: Internship in one of our partnered companies for practical.
  • Placement Readiness Training: Resume building, mock interviews, aptitude sessions, and soft skill development.
  • Placement Assistance: Dedicated support and connections to help you secure a role post-internship.
