Know more about Coincent Partnership Program

Applications for Coincent Partnership Program (CPP) are open to college-going students of all degrees, streams, and years. Any college student who is a marketing & networking enthusiast, and who wants to experience the excitement of a growing startup, can apply for this program.

As an CPP, your main role is to -
  • Promote Coincent Certification & internships among your peers and assist them with internship-related queries
  • Help your peers learn new skills via Coincent Trainings, certification & Internship.
  • Guide your peers to join the webinar for Certification and Internship
