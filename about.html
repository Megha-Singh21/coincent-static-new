About Us

Coincent is an EdTech SaaS platform founded in 2021, focused on bridging the gap between academics and industry. We partner with companies to deliver a 3-year, mentor-led program for undergraduates, offering hands-on training in AI, Data Science, VLSI, Full Stack Development, and more. With real-world projects and placement support, Coincent helps students become job-ready professionals.

HIGHLIGHTS IN COINCENT

Coincent's top structured learning model for students is the Coincent 3-Year Program.

The Coincent 3-Year Program is a long-term upskilling journey tailored for undergraduate students from Year 1 to Year 3/4. The program aligns with a student’s academic progression and is divided as follows:

  • Year 1 – Learn: Foundation building through industrial training, real-world tools, and expert mentorship.
  • Year 2 – Build: Development of capstone projects, live simulations, and domain specialization.
  • Year 3 – Lead: Career readiness through mock interviews, resume building, portfolio development, and placement assistance.
